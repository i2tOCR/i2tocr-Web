Privacy Policy

We're committed to protecting your privacy. Here's exactly how we handle your data.

Key Privacy Points

No Data Storage: We don't store your images, documents, or extracted text. All processing is real-time and data is discarded immediately.

Tesseract Engine: When using Tesseract.js, OCR runs entirely in your browser — your image never leaves your device.

Completely Free: I2TOCR is 100% free with no hidden costs or premium tiers.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how I2TOCR ("we," "us") collects, uses, and discloses information from users of our OCR web application and iOS app. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data Processing & Storage

We do not store any of the following:

  • Uploaded images or documents
  • Extracted text from your documents
  • Processing results or OCR outputs
  • User credentials or personal authentication data

All OCR processing occurs in real-time. Your data is immediately discarded after conversion is complete.

3. iOS App Authentication

Our iOS application uses Apple's built-in security framework:

  • Authentication via Face ID, Touch ID, or device passcode
  • We do not collect, store, or access your biometric data
  • No separate username/password system
  • All authentication occurs locally on your device

4. Service Model

I2TOCR is completely free to use. We do not offer premium tiers, in-app purchases, or subscription models.

5. Third Party Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share minimal information only with trusted service providers necessary to run the OCR service, or when legally required.
