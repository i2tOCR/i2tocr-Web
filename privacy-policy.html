Important Privacy Notes

No Data Storage: We do not store any of your images, documents, or extracted text on our servers. All processing happens in real-time and data is immediately discarded after processing.

iOS Authentication: In our iOS app, authentication is handled exclusively through your device's built-in security (Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode). We don't collect or store login credentials.

Completely Free: i2t OCR is a 100% free service with no hidden costs or premium tiers.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how i2t OCR ("we," "Company," or "us") collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users ("you") of our OCR App and website. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data Processing & Storage Policy

No Data Retention: We want to be absolutely clear - we do not store any of the following:

  • Uploaded images or documents
  • Extracted text from your documents
  • Processing results or OCR outputs
  • User credentials or personal authentication data

All OCR processing occurs in real-time, and your data is immediately discarded after the conversion is complete.

3. iOS App Authentication

For our iOS application, we utilize Apple's built-in security framework:

  • Authentication is handled through Face ID, Touch ID, or device passcode
  • We do not collect, store, or have access to your biometric data
  • No separate username/password system is required
  • All authentication occurs locally on your device

4. Service Model

Free Service: i2t OCR is completely free to use. We do not offer premium tiers, in-app purchases, or subscription models. Our service is supported without charging our users.

5. Disclosure of Personal Information to Third Parties

We do not sell your Personal Data. We share minimal information only with trusted service providers necessary to run the OCR service, or when legally required.
